Traveling to experience different cultures is important for me. Being of mixed backgrounds, I am fascinated with experiencing everything I can while I can! Learning about different foods is one of my favorite things about traveling, since I love to cook and bake.

Baking has been a big part of my life. Creating something from humble ingredients to share with people is heartwarming. I have worked a long time in the food industry, learning as much as I can, because it's always been a passion.
